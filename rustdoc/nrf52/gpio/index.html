GpioteRegisters

The nRF51822 doesn't automatically provide GPIO interrupts. Instead, to receive interrupts from a GPIO line, you must allocate a GPIOTE (GPIO Task and Event) channel, and bind the channel to the desired pin. There are 4 channels. This means that requesting an interrupt can fail, if there are already 4 allocated.
