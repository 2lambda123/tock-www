nrf52

Module nrf52::clock [−] [src]

The clock peripheral of the nRF51 series (chapter 13 of the nRF51 reference manual v3.0), which manages the low frequency and high frequency clocks. The low frequency clock drives the real time clock (RTC), while the high frequency clocks drive the timer system.
